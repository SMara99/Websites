# Habit Tracker

A modern, interactive habit tracking application that helps users build and maintain positive habits through visual progress tracking and customizable categories.

## Features

- **Multiple Tracking Frequencies**: Daily, weekly, and monthly habit options
- **Category Organization**: Organize habits into Health, Productivity, Mindfulness, Social, and Hobbies
- **Custom Habit Creation**: Add personalized habits with flexible tracking options
- **Visual Progress**: Track your streaks and completion rates
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices
- **Local Storage**: Your data persists across sessions

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Use the sidebar to filter habits by frequency or category
3. Click "+ Add New Habit" to create custom habits
4. Mark habits as complete by clicking on them
5. Track your progress over time
